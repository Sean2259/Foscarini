We Are Creative Agency That Create Beautiful Lights

Foscarini imagines, develops and produces decorative lamps: night and day, whether they’re on or off, they seduce, surprise and inspire.

Foscarini designs with an independence from any production constraints. The company’s 35 years of collaborations with international designers and mastery of lighting resulted in a long list of design icons integrated into domestic or collective environments globally.
